About Sangat National High School

Sangat National High School is a leading educational institution dedicated to providing quality secondary education. Established with the vision of nurturing well-rounded individuals, we combine academic excellence with character formation and skills development.

Our Mission

To provide quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education that empowers every learner to become productive and responsible citizens.

Our Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.
